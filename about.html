Our Mission

Help people find adorable cats safely and responsibly.

At Meow Market, we believe every cat deserves a loving home and every human deserves a purring best friend. Our platform curates listings from trusted, verified sellers and fosters to ensure health, safety, and happiness for both cats and adopters.

  • Verified, ethical sellers and fosters
  • Health and care information up-front
  • Simple, transparent communication
  • Education resources for new cat parents

Note: Meow Market is a demo site with no real transactions.
